Projects

Recapify

Recapify is a web application designed for Spotify users to gain insight into their listening habits, listing their top tracks, artists, and genres over multiple time frames, along with personalized song recommendations based on their listening activity. By integrating the Spotify API, Recapify allows users access their personal account data and observe their listening preferences over the past 1 month, 6 months, and 12 months, with 5 tailored song suggestions per period. This project was built using Python, CSS, HTML, and React.

FitFlow

FitFlow is an AI-powered fitness app created to deliver personalized workout and diet plans tailored to a user’s personal fitness goals. By leveraging AI, FitFlow creates custom 7-day fitness routines and nutrition suggestions to support every user’s unique journey to health and wellness. This app was built with Python, CSS, HTML, and React.
